FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What Is Your Rate?

Rates vary depending on the job site and equipment needed. Call, text or email us for a free project quote.

Are You Licensed, Bonded and Insured?

Yes! We are fully licensed, bonded and insured in the state of Washington.

What Areas Do You Service?

We service most areas in both Pierce County and Kitsap County including (but not limited to) Gig Harbor, Port Orchard, Key Peninsula, Ollala and Fox Island.

What Services Do You Provide?

We provide:

  • Site Prep (e.g. digging out foundations, site grading, site drainage, road building, etc.).
  • Dirt Work (e.g. grading, dirt removal, hauling, foundations, drainage, correction, etc.).
  • Land Clearing and Excavation (e.g. debris removal, stump removal, foundation cuts, etc.).
  • Demolition (e.g. house demolition, removal of any structures, hauling, etc.).

What Equipment Do You Have?

Our equipment includes:

  • A large excavator for demolition, clearing, etc.
  • A mini-excavator for many smaller dirt work, clearing projects, etc.
  • A tracked skid-steer (tracked loader) for many jobs such as grading, clearing, drive ways, etc.
  • A large 15-yard dump truck for big jobs along with a 5-yard dump truck.
  • Many other attachements, trailers, trucks and other equipment. Call, text or email us for details.

ABOUT US

JohnEdirt was founded in 2014 by then-student John Ellis. John, a truck enthusiast and budding entrepreneur began doing tractor work to help pay for school. He quickly realized that he had a knack for these services and a natural talent for tractor operation. Ellis’s father, a successful businessman, allowed John to use his basic Kubota tractor for small projects locally.
Within a couple months, JohnEdirt was born. John withdrew from college and used the money he had saved to purchase his very own tractor. JohnEdirt quickly became a household name in the area, due to his inspiring work ethic and incredible reputation.
Now, John continues to expand his business by acquiring more equipment and hiring more full and part-time employees. JohnEdirt’s market has expanded throughout Pierce and Kitsap counties.
